Audience Engagement Work

The Columbia Missourian, a community news organization managed by professional editors and staffed by Missouri School of Journalism students.

Election Alert Newsletter

In this audience project, I worked with a group to put together a weekly newsletter for The Columbia Missourian meant to inform and engage voters ahead of the General Municipal Election. This newsletter served as a form of service journalism, answering frequently asked questions about the upcoming election, providing summaries of forums and candidate profiles, as well as linking to reporting from The Missourian itself.
